Explore your cocktail database through interactive visualizations. These charts provide insights into your ingredient collection and cocktail recipes.

About Visualizations

This tab provides visual insights into your CocktailDB data:

  • Ingredients by Type: Shows the distribution of your ingredients across different categories (spirits, liqueurs, juices, etc.)
  • Stock Status: Displays the proportion of ingredients you currently have in stock versus those you need to purchase

More visualization types will be added in future updates to help you better understand your cocktail collection.
