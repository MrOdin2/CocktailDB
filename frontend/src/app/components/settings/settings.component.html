About Themes

The Basic theme is the default professional appearance.

Optional retro themes feature:

  • Authentic 80s color palettes with atmospheric backgrounds
  • Courier New monospace font for terminal authenticity
  • CRT scanline overlay effects
  • Text glow effects (phosphor screen simulation)
  • 80s-style blocky borders (no rounded corners)
  • High contrast for maximum readability

Your theme preference is automatically saved and will persist across sessions.
